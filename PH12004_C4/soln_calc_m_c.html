Solution: Calculate \(m\) and \(c\)#

From the equations for \(m\) and \(c\) in the worksheet:

N = len(bolt_df.time) ## we also need the number of points

m = ((N * sum_x_y) - (sum_x*sum_y))/((N * sum_x_2) - (sum_x)**2)
c = (sum_y - m*sum_x)/N

print(m,c)
